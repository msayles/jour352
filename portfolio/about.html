About Me:

My name is Megan Sayles, and I am a junior at the University of Maryland in College Park. I am currently pursuing a degree in both multiplatform journalism and government and politics. I expect to graduate in May 2021.

In addition to my school work, I am currently interning at Baltimore magazine, a monthly print publication with a regularly updated website. There, I am an editorial intern. Some of the things I do, include fact-checking articles, contributing to online stories, writing event listings, and writing metro stories for the print magazine.

Besides my internship, I have written and copy edited for miscellaneous publications on campus, including Stories Beneath the Shell, The Black Explosion, Unwind Magazine, and Her Campus. I'm also a university tour guide. Additionally, last summer, I freelanced for my town newspaper, The Pasadena Voice.

In my free time, I like to spend time with my friends and family. I love playing the piano and singing, and I pretty much listen to music 24/7. I also enjoy trying new food spots and playing sports.
